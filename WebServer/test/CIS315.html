  • Late policy for 315: All written assignments are due by 4:45 pm of the stated due date, though it is much safer to turn them in at class time. You may turn them in via Canvas (photos of handwritten work not accepted) or there is a drop box, where you can put your assignments, on the first floor of Deschutes Hall. Programming assignments are due by midnight of the due date and can only be submitted via Canvas. Assignments will be accepted for up to three days past the due date with a penalty of 5% per day.

  • Grading: Please note that work done correctly and meeting the specifications of the assignment will receive a base score of about 80%. Higher scores will be reserved for work that is more complete and/or especially elegant.

  • Attendance: More than 5 unexcused absences, or 3 consecutive ones, may negatively affect your final grade.
